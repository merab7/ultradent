Services

Therapeutic dentistry

Caries: includes mechanical and medical treatment of the damaged tooth cavity, followed by its restoration with modern helio braces. We use only the latest technologies and materials, which ensure maximum tooth protection and natural appearance. Complications of caries: include mechanical and medical treatment of tooth root canals, filling the canal with modern fillers (sealer, gutta-percha, etc.) and subsequent restoration of the damaged crown. This process provides long-term protection and strengthening of the tooth.

Restoration

It includes restoration of tooth shape, color and other damages with maximum aesthetic adaptation. Our goal is to provide our patients with high-quality aesthetic services that promote their self-confidence and improve their smile.

Surgical services

Includes simple and complex extraction, extraction of retentive teeth, implantation, resection and gingivectomy. Our surgical procedures are performed with maximum precision and focus on patient comfort.

Children's stomatology

Includes surgical, therapeutic and orthodontic services. Our specialists ensure the health and proper development of children's teeth in a friendly and safe environment.

Orthodontics

Includes treatment with removable appliances, braces, aligners and caps. We offer patients modern orthodontic solutions that ensure proper alignment of teeth and a healthy smile.

Orthopedic dentistry

Includes removable dentures (rigid and flexible), Bügel dentures with attachmen and interlocks. Our prostheses are designed with high quality materials and provide maximum comfort. Includes non-removable structures: zircono-ceramic crowns and bridges, pre-ceramics and veneers, metal-ceramic crowns and bridges. Our experience and technologies ensure the perfect functionality and aesthetics of the patients' teeth.
